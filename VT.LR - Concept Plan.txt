SHORT SYNOPSIS OF VT/LR

	VETRA(VT) is Vietra Galaxy and is my main avatar for VRChat - 2018
	
		Vietra existed within a cloud of purple vapour surrounded by the blackness of space.
		she grew and lived for billions of years until awakening recently due to being..
		Called out by somthing, they called her Vetra or the Purple Wind.
		The girl with eyes for a galaxy.


	LONELY ROAD(LR) is a world i worked on within Roblox since - 2013
	
		Lonely Road was based in an alternate universe where all time periods came into one
		dimension, this dimension was known as the Akriah dimension. storys and rumours tell
		that when traveling somewhere out south of the atlantic ocean a time gate would open 
		pulling anything in its sourroundings and ejecting it into the Akriah dimension, this
		was known as the Lonely Road due to the fact that half of the Atlantic was blocked
		off from the rest of the society.


	VRCWORLD(VT-LR) is a combo of VT & LR to form a great story - 2018 

		Its Year 3307, August 17th. a Class-A 8601 Chronos space ship jumps to light speed at
		warp factor 6. Only 11 minutes into the trip the Celeron hyperdrive within the ship 
		malfunctions causing the CHD lock frame to jam into active mode which prevents the
		warp core from shutting down. Eventually, the CHD overheats causing a nuclear
		meltdown pulling the craft out of jump. And crash landing on an unknown planet.
